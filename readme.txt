Provides both widgets and shortcodes to help you display top rated shortfilms  on your website.  The official Shortfundly shortfilm plugin. 


== Description ==

Shortfundly is an online technology and data driven platform company that curates and shares the best short films and stories through our global multi-platform network. Our video and editorial platform enables a global audience to easily discover, watch and share unique stories anywhere on their desktop and mobile devices.

Our vision is to continually grow the community of short film audience to enable a wide and accessible market reach for short films and become the leading micro-cinema/short film platform. These plugins are great to add to your personal web site to show off what are most viewed in filmmakers community like shortfundly, and more and more audience in the industry are starting to stay connected with creative shortfilms from our shortfundly passionate filmmakers.

This plugin allows you to show off the top rated shortfilms from our shortfundly filmmakers creative platform and display them on your WordPress site.  You can use a widget to display our latest interesting most viewed shortfilms in a header, sidebar or footer or use a shortcode to display most viewed shortfilms in the main content are for a post or page. 

This is the official shortfilm plugin for shortfundly to enable filmmakers creative short films to reach next level of audience.

== Installation ==

There are a few options for installing and setting up this plugin.

= Upload Manually =

1. Download and unzip the plugin
2. Upload the 'shortfundly-plugin' folder into the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
3. Go to the Plugins admin page and activate the plugin

= Install Via Admin Area =

1. In the admin area go to Plugins > Add New and search for "Shortfundly Plugin"
2. Click install and then click activate

= To Setup The Plugin =

1. Get your shortfundly X-API-Key (see instructions under FAQ).
2. In the WordPress admin area go to Settings > Top Rated films and then enter in your X-API-Key.

= How to Use the Widget =

1. Setup the Plugin (refer to above)
2. Go to Appearance > Widgets and drag the 'Shortfundly shortfilms Widget' to your sidebar.  
3. Enter in a Title to appear above the shortfilms.  For example "Top-rated shortfilms"

= How to Use the Shortcode =

1. Navigate to the post or page you would like to add the badges to
2. Enter in the shortcode [shortfundly-plugin]
3. By default, 5 top rated shortfilms are displayed.
4. You can navigate through more shortfilms by clicking '>' or '<' buttons below the films in the website.
